## Project #56 — AIM: Hairpin and Battery Busbar Laser Welding (added 2026-04-17)
Electric motor hairpin winding joints and battery busbar interconnects in EVs require precise, low-resistance, zero-porosity welds. CMA's AIM project investigates laser welding (including blue/green wavelengths for copper absorption), resistance welding, and micro-TIG welding to find optimal processes for high-volume EV drivetrain manufacturing. Key challenges: welding copper with low IR absorption, dissimilar metal joints (Cu-Al), tight geometric tolerances. Currently work in progress with automotive industry partners.
